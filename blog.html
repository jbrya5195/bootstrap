Black History reincarnated

It was previously reported that the Kendrick Lamar and J. Cole collaboration album is still in the works but the two artists are focusing primarily on their individual careers. However, a new report from Complex hints that the album is being delayed because of an upcoming summer tour that will see J. Cole and Drake team up. "There's a rumor that I heard that there's gonna be an announcement this week of a Drake and J. Cole summer tour," stated Ebro Darden of Hot 97. Unfortunately, neither Drake nor J. Cole's camp was able to verify if this statement was true or not.
